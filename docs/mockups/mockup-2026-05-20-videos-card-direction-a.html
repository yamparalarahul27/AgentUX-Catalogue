Videos section — card ideation

Three card layouts for X-post references in the Videos section. Goal: cleaner UI, no inline X embed on first render, click-to-load. Currently each card renders the full twttr.widgets.createTweet iframe inline — heavy on the network and visually cluttered. The two proposed directions show what a thumbnail-first card looks like.
