How to read the difference

The squircle effect is intentionally subtle — most visible on larger surfaces (the media frame, the composer) and on small frequently-seen elements (action buttons, send pill) where the rounded square shape registers most strongly. Look at the corner of the screenshot frame in both blocks: the Current block's corner has a clear inflection point where straight edge meets curve; the Lisse block's corner eases continuously into the curve. The same easing applies to every rounded surface in the panel — composer, send, icon buttons, meta icon — adding up to a noticeably more "designed" feel without any layout change.

  • Big surfaces (media frame, composer): difference most visible — corner radius 16-20px
  • Buttons (close, action toolbar, send): small radius (~9-12px) but huge repetition cost — Lisse compounds across 9 buttons per lightbox
  • Inline avatars / meta icons: 6-8px radius — squircle starts to read like the iOS app icon shape
